Stop distances are fine-tuned using Maximum Adverse Excursion (MAE) analysis — each instrument's stop is calibrated to the statistical distribution of intrabar adverse moves to minimise unnecessary stop-outs while preserving the edge. Lot sizes shown are at full base risk with no decay applied. Actual lots will vary with the live stop distance and current risk decay state.

Important: These calculations assume standard MT5 broker contract sizes. If your broker uses non-standard sizing (e.g. mini contracts) verify your first trade on each instrument before going live.
